Visit Jalandhar

Explore Jalandhar through its vibrant Punjabi culture, flavorful local cuisine, and bustling sports goods markets that reflect the city’s energetic spirit.

Top three activities to do at Jalandhar

Explore Local Cuisine

Savor iconic Punjabi dishes like chole bhature, Amritsari kulcha, and refreshing lassi. Don’t miss the famous Dhanna’s Kachori, known for serving delicious, filling kachoris for over 200 years.

Visit Historic and Spiritual Sites

Explore important landmarks such as Jang-e-Azadi Memorial, Khatkar Kalan, and Devi Talab Mandir, one of the revered 50 Shakti Peeths in India.

Experience Village Life

Discover the vibrant village lifestyle, from traditional sports to everyday celebrations. The warmth and hospitality of the locals will leave you with unforgettable memories.
